Welcome!

Whether you are just beginning your tennis journey,
or you are an experienced player, new strings are a part of
the game. Don't let the inconvenience of a string job keep you off the court!
We allow you to determine the most convenient days and times for the job to be completed.
Here, your racket can be (1) picked up and delivered by us, or (2) dropped off
to the stringer by you, the user!



Our Mission

At Tennis Twine, our goal is to get your racket stringed up and back to you in the most convenient manner. As an OU student, this is an opportunity for me to support the surrounding communities while earning funds and gaining experience. Here, you set the pick-up and delivery location as well as determine when the job gets done!

Our Service

We recognize that there are plenty of racket stringing services from which you can choose. At Tennis Twine, we hope to improve the experience by coming to you; we will do the traveling and stringing by the date that you set. Aside from this, we offer lessons on that new set of strings at whichever courts are convenient for you! We would appreciate any feedback on the process as well!

How to Get Started

To begin, all that you need to do is press the start button! Begin by sharing your contact information including your address if you would like the pick-up/delivery service. Following this, give us the specifics of the desired string job like tension and string type, date of transfer, and your interest in a lesson.
