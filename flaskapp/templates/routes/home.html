Welcome!

Whether you are just beginning your tennis journey, or you are an experienced player, new strings are a part of the game.
Don't let the inconvenience of a string job keep you off the court!
We allow you to determine the most convenient dates and times for the job to be completed.
Here, your racket can be (1) picked up and dropped off by us, or (2) dropped off to the stringer by you, the user!

Our Mission

At Tennis Twine, our goal is to get your racket stringed up and back to you in the most convenient manner. As an OU student, this is an opportunity for me to support the surrounding communities while earning funds and gaining experience.

Our Service

At Tennis Twine, we hope to improve the restringing experience by coming to you; we will do the traveling and stringing based on the pick up and drop off dates and times you provide. We offer lessons on that new set of strings at whichever courts are convenient for you!

How to Get Started

To begin, all that you need to do is press the start button! Begin by sharing your contact information including your address if you would like the pick-up/delivery service. Give us the specifics like string type, tension, date of transfer, and your interest in a lesson.
