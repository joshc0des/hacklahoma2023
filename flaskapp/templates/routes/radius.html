Radius of Travel

Press here to visit an online radius mapping application. Use this to find your location
in relationship to the 20 mile radius. Type in the address below upon arriving to the website to create the area range:

409 W Boyd St,
Norman, OK 73069

After entering this address, select the "Radius Miles" drop-down menu. From here select the "20 mi"
option. You now have a circle with a 20 mile radius displayed on the map!

Now, simply type in the address of the requested pick-up / delivery location. This action will zoom into the newly entered address which
you can then expand your view to see if the location is within the circle that was created. If the location is within the circle, then
the service will be the flate rate $5 which includes both the pick-up and the delivery. If this is not the case, then there
will be an additional 75 cent charge for each additional mile traveled!
