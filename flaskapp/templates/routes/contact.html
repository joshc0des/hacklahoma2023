Radius of Travel

A flat fee of $5 is charged for the delivery service within a 20 mile radius of the OU campus in Norman.

Within the radius includes:
  • Blanchard
  • Cole
  • Goldsby
  • Moore
  • Newcastle
  • Noble
  • Norman
  • Purcell
  • Slaughterville
  • Valley Brook
  • Washington
And parts of both Mustang and southern Oklahoma City
